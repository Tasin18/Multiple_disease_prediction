Here are some prevention techniques for Breast Cancer:

  • Eat a healthy and balanced diet. Aim for a body mass index (BMI) within the healthy range.
  • Choose a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, lean proteins, and healthy fats.
  • Limit alcohol intake. Research suggests that even moderate alcohol consumption may increase the risk of breast cancer.
  • Avoid Hormone Replacement Therapy (HRT)
  • Regular breast cancer screenings, such as mammograms and clinical breast exams, can help detect cancer in its early stages when it is more treatable.
  • Minimize exposure to environmental toxins and radiation.
