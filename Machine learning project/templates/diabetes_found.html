Here are some remedies for Diabetes:

  1. Follow a Healthy Eating Plan. Include a variety of nutrient-rich foods, such as fruits, vegetables, whole grains, lean proteins, and healthy fats.
  2. Engage in regular exercise, such as walking, swimming, or cycling.
  3. Maintain a Healthy Weight.
  4. Regularly monitor blood glucose levels as advised by your healthcare team.
  5. Take prescribed medications as directed.
  6. Practice stress-reducing techniques such as deep breathing, meditation, or yoga.
